Purpose

To bring people from all walks of life and welcome them in our shop so they can relax, get comfortable and enjoy our delicious meals and couisine prepared by our top master chef's from all over the world. Audience

Feeling hungry? Tired? or Bored/Stressed by the day to day running activities?...Then come on in to our cafe shop and take the load off with our top of the range facilities. We provide services from coffee, lunch meals and uninterrupted wifi services for you to surf the web and internet as you catch up on the daily news and social media. Everyone is welcome to come and dine with us even little kids as we have special games for kids to play while you enjoy your food. We have got you covered. Couples are also invited as we set the mood for a special date with romantic songs and lighting.

Home

We at Kazembe Coffee and Cafe would like to extend our warmest regards for visiting our site. We are a family owned restaurant established in 2018 by our late loving father whose vision was to serve people from around our community with the best food and drinks and ensure customer satisfaction is guaranteed.

We want to serve as a center of social interaction and provide our patrons with a place to congregate, talk read, write, entertain one another or pass the time, whether individually or in small groups. For those sitting in, we provide them with free internet to browse and catch up on their social media handles while enjoying our delicious food. We also created a play park for your adorable kids to play in and not disturb you so everyone is invited For individuals who are in a hurry to travel abroad or going to work, we have a drive-thru where you can get served on-the-go and not worry about long standing queues.

We are open everyday from 7:30am-5:30pm Mon-Fri and 8:00am-6:00pm Sat-Sun. We dont take bookings but we have tables available inside and outside for walk-ins. We are located at number 23 Jefferson Street Cnr 4a-5a Perrins Court Harare. Feel free to get in touch with us over the phone: +263771666946 or on our email address: www.kazembecafe41@gmail.com

Menu

We serve coffee, espresso, latte and cappuccino. We also offer cold drinks such as iced coffee and iced tea, as well as other non-caffeinated beverages. We also serve food such as light snacks, sandwiches, muffins, fruit or pastries. Some customers also come in and ask for alcoholic beverages, we do serve those as well. We also serve full on lunch meals and dessert whether you are looking for a sweet treat or dessert with friends, we got you covered.

Our prices are one of the best in town as we are relatively affordable to meet your budget and not overspend. We do offer specials on early birds who get a 50% discount from 8am-9am daily. We also have a pensioners special who get a 50% discount off sit-in diners over the age of 65 years and above on Thursdays Every monday we add new and exciting dishes to our menu to cater for everyone but do not worry all your favorites are still there too. Come through and indulge.

Orders & Bookings

Customers can come and place their bookings on tables and orders on food as we would like to serve people online who cannot make it in person due to unforseen circumstances. This way customers are not left out of the joy and experience of enjoying our wide variety of delicious food and couisine. It only takes a few minutes to fill out the form and our team will respond to your feedback in moments as they are readily available to cater to evey customer needs. We also offer FREE DELIVERY to orders above $50 and to customers who are within a certain radius of about 2km from us. Its an added advantage to be located near us!.
